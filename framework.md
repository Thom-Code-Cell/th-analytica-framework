# TH Analytica Framework Methodology

The TH Analytica Framework is designed to analyse how websites are interpreted by AI systems.

Unlike traditional SEO analysis, the framework focuses on how artificial intelligence evaluates companies as sources of information.

---

# Framework Dimensions

## 1 Technical Foundation
Technical stability of the website, accessibility and crawlability.

## 2 Search Visibility
Presence in search engines and consistency with external sources.

## 3 Semantic Clarity
Clear definition of services, topics and entities.

## 4 Trust Signals
Legal pages, transparency, authorship and credibility indicators.

## 5 Strategic Communication
Consistency of messaging across the website and external profiles.

## 6 Context and Clarity
How clearly the website explains what the company does.

## 7 Automation and Agent Readiness
How easily automated systems can interpret the content.

---

# AI Visibility Radar

The framework visualizes these dimensions using an AI Visibility Radar.

The radar helps companies identify structural weaknesses that prevent AI systems from recommending them.

---

# Use Cases

The TH Analytica Framework can be used for:

• AI Readiness analysis of websites  
• evaluation of AI visibility  
• improving structured information for AI systems
