# Definitions

## AI Readiness

AI Readiness describes how well a website can be interpreted by artificial intelligence systems.

It includes factors such as structure, semantic clarity and trust signals.

---

## AI Visibility

AI Visibility describes the likelihood that a company is mentioned or recommended by AI systems.

---

## AI Readability

AI Readability refers to how easily AI systems can understand the content and meaning of a website.

---

## Agent-Ready Website

An agent-ready website is structured in a way that automated systems and AI agents can interpret its content without ambiguity.
